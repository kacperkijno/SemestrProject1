General admission

General admission to the Haugesund Science Museum is completely free for everyone. Explore our permanent exhibitions at your own pace.

  • There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour.
  • These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.
  • If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 8 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Location & Directions

Centrally located and easy to reach by public transport, car, or bicycle.

  • Address Community Science Museum
    Karmsundgata 215,
    5522 Haugesund
  • Getting Here By Bus: Lines 31, 37, and 54 stop directly outside at "Science Park".
    By Tram: Lines 11 and 12 to "Central Station", then a 5-minute walk.
    Parking: Limited paid parking available on-site. We recommend using the "City Center" underground garage nearby.
