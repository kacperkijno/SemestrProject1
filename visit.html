General admission

FREE ENTRY

General admission to the Haugesund Science Museum is completely free for everyone. Explore our permanent exhibitions at your own pace.

Guided Tours

  • There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour.
  • These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.
  • If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 8 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Location & Directions

Centrally located and easy to reach by public transport, car, or bicycle.

Getting Here

By Bus

Lines 31, 37, and 54 stop directly outside at "Science Park".

By Tram

Lines 11 and 12 to "Central Station", then a 5-minute walk.

Parking

Limited paid parking available on-site. We recommend using the "City Center" underground garage nearby.

Museum Facilities

Accessibility

We are committed to making science accessible to everyone.

  • Full wheelchair access
  • Free audio guides
  • Braille signage throughout

Food & Drink

Take a break and recharge at our on-site café.

  • Light meals & sandwiches
  • Hot and cold beverages
  • Kid-friendly snack options

Museum Shop

Take the discovery home with you from our curated gift shop.

  • Educational gifts & books
  • Interactive science kits
  • Exclusive HSM souvenirs
